The philosophy

Authentic stories.
Never posed.
Always felt.

KS Your Style Photo is Karen Savegnago's documentary approach to corporate and brand photography. Working invisibly through your event, Karen captures the unguarded instants — the energy, the connection, the atmosphere — that define who your organisation truly is.

From conference floors to product launches, brand activations to executive summits, Karen's lens reveals not just what happened, but how it felt. The result is imagery that speaks directly to your audience, because it is completely, unapologetically real.

The approach

How Karen works

01

Purely Candid

No posing. No direction. No forced smiles. Karen moves through your event unseen, capturing moments that feel authentic because they are — raw, human, and entirely real.

02

Behind the Scenes

The preparation backstage. The spontaneous laughter. The focused quiet before a keynote. BTS storytelling reveals the depth behind your brand and builds genuine audience trust.

03

Atmospheric Identity

Every brand has an energy. Karen translates that intangible feeling — the buzz, the warmth, the intensity — into powerful imagery that captures the soul of your organisation.
